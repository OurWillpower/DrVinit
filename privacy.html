Last updated: 2025

Overview

Shree Varad Maternity ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you use our website at shreevaradmaternity.com (or our GitHub Pages site).

Information we collect

How we use information

  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services (for example SendGrid to deliver appointment emails, Google Analytics for website analytics). These services have their own privacy policies and we recommend reviewing them.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your rights
